# 🍪 New Recipe — Fill-In Form (Made by Abi)

Copy everything below the line, fill in the blanks, and send it back. No code needed —
just replace the prompts in `< >`. A developer adds it to the app, and after the next
deploy it shows up on **every** device automatically.

Tips for filling it out:
- **Weights in grams, oven in °C** (conventional — the app shows the fan-forced temp
  automatically). Use Australian ingredient names (plain flour, bicarb soda, caster
  sugar, etc.).
- **Ingredients:** one per line as `amount | unit | name | note`. Units are usually
  `g`, `ml`, `tsp`, `tbsp`, or left blank for whole items (like eggs). Use `0` as the
  amount for "to finish"/to taste items (e.g. flaky salt).
  Examples: `230 | g | unsalted butter |`  ·  `2 | | eggs | large`  ·
  `0 | | flaky sea salt | to finish`
- **Steps:** one per line. If a step needs a timer, add `(timer: 12 min)` at the end.
  For stuffed cookies, include a "freeze the filling" step and a "wrap dough around the
  frozen filling" step.
- **Yield** is roughly how many cookies one batch makes (used for batch scaling).
- **Contains** lists allergens shown on the recipe, e.g. `Gluten, Dairy, Egg, Soy`.
